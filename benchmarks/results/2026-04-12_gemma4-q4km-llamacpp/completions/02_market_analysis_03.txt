**Signal Identification — The RRG Quadrant Map:**

Technology (XLK) in Weakening, heading into Lagging: This is the most bearish signal in the set. Technology is losing relative strength against the SPX and is the sector most likely to underperform. The heading into Lagging means the decline in relative strength is accelerating.

Industrials (XLI) and Energy (XLE) in Improving with strong clockwise momentum: These sectors are gaining relative strength and doing so with increasing velocity. The clockwise rotation from Weakening → Improving → Leading is the bullish signal. The fact that both are in the same quadrant with similar momentum suggests a broad risk-on rotation.

Utilities (XLU) just rotated from Improving into Leading: This is the most interesting signal. Utilities rotating into Leading is typically a defensive rotation — it means investors are seeking safety. But XLU is entering Leading while XLI and XLE are also in Improving. This creates a tension: the market is rotating into defensive sectors (XLU) while simultaneously rotating into cyclical sectors (XLI, XLE). This tension is the key to the 6-week sideways SPX chop — the market is indecisive.

Financials (XLF) in Leading with decelerating momentum: Financials have been the relative strength leader but are now losing steam. This is the early warning that the rotation into cyclicals (XLI, XLE) may be replacing the rotation into financials.

DXY up 3.2% over 6 weeks: This is a critical macro input. A strong dollar is typically bearish for cyclicals and commodities (XLE), but it's also bearish for Technology (XLK) — so XLK's Weakening could be driven by the dollar rather than sector-specific weakness. This means XLK's decline may be a dollar-driven phenomenon rather than structural.

**Framework Application — Murphy's Relative Strength Rotation Method:**

Murphy's framework for relative strength rotation is clear: sectors that are gaining relative strength are the ones to hold, and sectors losing relative strength should be sold. The RRG provides a visual and quantifiable way to implement this. The clockwise rotation from Weakening → Improving → Leading is the key pattern. XLI and XLE are in the early stages of this rotation and represent the highest-probability long entries.
