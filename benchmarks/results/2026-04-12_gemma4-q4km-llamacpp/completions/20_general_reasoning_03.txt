**Model 3: Page's Many-Model Framework — Aggregating the Lenses**
Applying Page's method of combining multiple models to identify consensus and disagreement: the bull case (AI demand, superior chip performance) and the bear case (debt, margin compression, activist conflict) are both valid models. **Model 4: Munger's Circle of Competence — What Don't You Know?**
