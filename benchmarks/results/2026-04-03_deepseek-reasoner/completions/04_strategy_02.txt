### **2. TRADE MECHANICS**  

#### **A. Entry Trigger**  
- **Primary Condition:** Wait for a **4-hour close above $521.00** (weekly triangle resistance).  
- **Secondary Confirmation:** Volume on breakout should exceed Monday’s volume (1.8x avg daily) to validate momentum.  
- **Order:** Buy stop limit order at **$521.10** (limit $521.50) after first 4-hour candle tests $521.

#### **B. Stop Loss**  
- **Location:** $514.20 (just below Monday’s bull engulfing low and the 20-day EMA).  
- **Rationale:** A drop below this level invalidates the daily structure and suggests failed breakout.  
- **Distance:** $6.90 risk per share from entry ($521.10 – $514.20).

#### **C. Profit Targets**  
- **PT1 (Partial Exit – Measured Move):** $534.80  
  - Calculated from triangle height: $521 – $492 = $29 projected move above breakout.  
  - $521 + $29 = $550 full target, but first resistance near 1/2 height at ~$534–535 (prior weekly highs).  
  - Exit 50% of position here.

- **PT2 (Trailing Stop – Momentum Capture):**  
  - After PT1 hit, trail remaining position with a **daily close below the 5-day SMA**.  
  - Alternatively, use a 2-bar low on the 4-hour chart (e.g., close below prior two 4-hour candle lows) for tighter exit.
